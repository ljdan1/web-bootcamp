Addis Ababa University

Addis Ababa University (Amharic: አዲስ አበባ ዩኒቨርሲቲ), or AAU, is an Ethiopian higher institute in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The main campus is located in the Arada subcity, 6 killo area.

Currently, AAU has enrolled more than 50000 undergraduate (under regular and continuing education program) and close to 15000 postgraduate students in Applied science, Social science, Health science and Enginneering and other fields. AAU is a university in the making, and much of its short-term plans aim at establishing academic infrastructures and facilities, staff recruitment and manpower development So far, we managed to recruit more than 15000 academic staff and administrative staff.[1][2]
